Test procedure for Paris rev A 2012-05-10
=========================================

Date:_____________________________________

Serial:___________________________________

1.) Test power. Red power LED should light. +5V on expansion pin 12

2.) CAN  Transmit.

3.) CAN  Receiver.

4.) Init Button.

5.) Green Init LED.

6.) Relay 0 - Red LED. +5V on expansion pin 3.

7.) Relay 1 - Red LED. +5V on expansion pin 4.

8.) Relay 2 - Red LED. +5V on expansion pin 5.

9.) Relay 3 - Red LED. +5V on expansion pin 6.

10.) Relay 4 - Red LED. +5V on expansion pin 7.

11.) Relay 5 - Red LED. +5V on expansion pin 8.

12.) Relay 6 - Red LED. +5V on expansion pin 9.

------------------------------------------------------ 
               Continue if all pass
------------------------------------------------------

13.) Program firmware.

14.) Program GUID.

15.) Test start.

16.) Clear EEPROM.

17.) Attach label.
